Auto Services

Tinting your car, truck or SUV is one of the best decisions you can make.

Enjoy the Ride

Tinting your car, truck or SUV is one of the best decisions you can make for two reasons: Health (skin cancer prevention) and Comfort (heat rejection in Richmond’s summer is a must).

Sun Block employees have been tinting cars since 1992. We use lifetime warranty film from Llumar. This warranty is nationwide and covers most anything that can happen to the film, including cracking, peeling, bubbling, or color change. We use Llumar’s software Precision Cut to cut the pattern of your specific vehicle. We have all 50 states tinting rules and laws to help guide you to a legal tint for the state where your car is registered. We use the same visible light transmission meter as law enforcement officials to ensure your vehicle is legal. We can also check your existing tint to make sure you are with-in legal limits.
We provide assistance in getting you the Ultimate Car Tint : obtaining a medical waiver and registering you vehicle for “Sun Shading” with DMV so you can get Air Blue 80 on your windshield. Air Blue 80 is a clear (80% visible light transmission) that rejects an amazing 43% of the heat coming trough the windshield. The medical waiver is an official DMV form you can download HERE

Have your dermatologist sign the medical waiver using skin cancer prevention as the reason. If you do not have a dermatologist, we suggest you visit Dermatology Associates of Virginia, P.C.
